## Features 🚀
Knoux Shield offers a suite of advanced features designed with security in mind. Below are some of the key functionalities:

### 🔍 Real-Time Threat Detection
Knoux Shield uses **AI** to monitor system activities and behaviors, identifying threats like malware, network intrusions, and targeted attacks in real-time. This ensures immediate response to potential risks.

### 🔐 Advanced Data Encryption
We employ **AES-256 encryption** to secure sensitive data, both in storage and during transmission, ensuring that only authorized users can access protected information.

### 📲 Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
Knoux Shield supports multiple layers of authentication, such as **OTP (One-Time Password)** or **biometric features** like fingerprint scanning, adding extra protection against unauthorized access.

### 🌐 Privacy Management
This feature allows users full control over their personal data. With strict privacy practices, sensitive information is **encrypted locally** and **never stored on external servers**, ensuring your data remains private.

### ☁️ Cloud Backup & Recovery
Secure, encrypted backups allow users to store their data safely in the cloud. In the event of data loss, recovery is seamless without risking unauthorized access.

### ⚡ Automated Security Updates
Knoux Shield periodically updates its security protocols to stay ahead of emerging cyber threats. Patches are applied automatically to ensure your system is always protected.

### 🎨 Customizable UI
The user interface is fully customizable to match user preferences, including support for **dark mode** and other personalization options.

### 🔒 Data Privacy & User Control
One of the core features of **Knoux Shield** is its ability to provide users with control over their own data. We believe that **privacy** should be a user's right, not a privilege, and offer several ways for users to ensure their information is kept safe.

- **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)**: All communications, whether they are between devices or with the cloud, are encrypted end-to-end to ensure that only the intended recipient can decrypt and access the data.
- **Secure Data Deletion**: If users choose to delete sensitive data, Knoux Shield ensures that data is securely wiped, making it unrecoverable by any means.
- **Anonymous Usage**: Users can choose to use the app with **minimal data exposure** to the system, ensuring that only necessary permissions are granted.

---

### 📊 Analytics & Performance Monitoring
Knoux Shield includes robust tools for monitoring system performance and detecting anomalies.

- **System Health Monitoring**: Continuously tracks CPU, RAM, and storage usage to alert the user of any abnormal behavior that could indicate a potential security threat or system compromise.
- **Real-Time Threat Analytics**: Provides dynamic analytics that assess the current threat landscape, categorizing and prioritizing alerts based on severity and risk.
- **Performance Optimization Tools**: A built-in optimization tool to improve system performance by identifying resource-hungry processes and offering actionable recommendations.

---

### 🌍 Multi-Device Synchronization
Knoux Shield is designed to work seamlessly across multiple platforms. Users can sync data and security settings across their **Android**, **iOS**, and **Windows** devices.

- **Cloud Integration**: Knoux Shield supports synchronization with cloud-based storage solutions, ensuring that all devices are up-to-date with the latest settings, patches, and configurations.
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Users can secure and monitor their digital lives, no matter the device they are using, with the same level of protection and functionality.

### 🛡️ Security Audits & Penetration Testing
Security is at the heart of Knoux Shield, and to maintain the highest standards, we conduct regular **security audits** and **penetration tests** on both the app and the underlying systems.

- **Vulnerability Assessment**: Regular checks are performed to identify potential vulnerabilities that could be exploited by attackers.
- **Red Team Exercises**: Our team conducts red team operations to simulate real-world attacks and assess the system’s ability to detect and respond to these threats.

### 🌐 Support for Enterprise Deployments
For businesses and enterprises, Knoux Shield offers an **enterprise-grade security solution**, providing scalable deployment options for large-scale teams.

- **Admin Dashboard**: A central console that allows administrators to manage device policies, monitor security status, and enforce security standards across the organization.
- **Compliance Reporting**: Knoux Shield helps enterprises maintain compliance with various industry standards (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA) by generating detailed reports for audits and assessments.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ❓

**Q1: How does Knoux Shield ensure real-time threat detection?**
- Knoux Shield utilizes **AI-based algorithms** to constantly monitor system activities. When it detects suspicious behavior or known attack patterns, it immediately triggers an alert and takes corrective actions to neutralize the threat.

**Q2: Is my data stored in the cloud, and who has access to it?**
- No. Data is **never stored in plain text** in the cloud. We use **end-to-end encryption** to ensure that only you have access to your data. Our team does not have the ability to decrypt or view your information.

**Q3: Can I use Knoux Shield on multiple devices?**
- Yes, Knoux Shield supports **multi-device synchronization**. Whether you're on Android, iOS, or Windows, you can protect and manage your data seamlessly across all devices.
